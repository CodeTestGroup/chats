Chat Guidelines

  1. Be Kind and Respectful: Treat others with kindness and respect.
  2. No Swearing or Offensive Language: Refrain from using profanity, hate speech, or any language that could be considered offensive.
  3. No Spamming or Flooding: Avoid excessive messaging, repeated posting of the same content, or flooding the chat with irrelevant information.
  4. Stay on Topic: Keep the conversation relevant to the chat's purpose.
  5. Respect Privacy: Do not share personal information about yourself or others in the chat.
  6. No Trolling or Harassment: Do not engage in trolling, harassment, or bullying behavior towards other users.
  7. Follow Instructions from Moderators: Follow any instructions given by chat moderators.
  8. Report Inappropriate Behavior: If you encounter any inappropriate behavior or content, report it to the moderators immediately.
  9. Have Fun and Be Engaging: Chat is a place for interaction and socializing.
  10. Stay Civil: Even in disagreements, maintain a civil and constructive tone.
